# I wrote you that last time

I wrote you that last time  
telling you I did not love you  
It was not time for us  
You were not ready  
I was not ready  
It was not right  
We were not perfect  
  
So I moved on and you let me  
  
Except that  
What I meant  
Instead of "not"  
was "now"
